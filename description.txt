Warhammer Items is a W.I.P. mod that aims to add some iconic artifacts from warhammer fantasy and some original complementary items and armor sets.

These items are mainly based on TW3's depictions.

Current Items:

Tzeentchian Staff
Khornate Pickaxe and Axe
Avelorn Bow
The Widowmaker
Skryre Warplock Rifle
Warpstone Bullets
